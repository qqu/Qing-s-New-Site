Brainstorming & Wireframe

Brainstorming is a powerful tool to understand user needs deeply. Every team member has the opportunity to make a contribution during this process, the initial design would be more comprehensive as well.
Wireframing provides us with a visible way to discuss the functionality and basic layout. In addition, users will be able to understand the product faster when we offer them this visible wireframing.

Prototype

I love prototype, because it is one of the most efficient way to communicate with users and team members. They are able to experience the workflow and find problems during the process.
Usually, on this step, I do 4-5 iterations to design prototype from low fidelity to high fidelity. Participants will be involved in each iteration to do the test and improve the user experience.
